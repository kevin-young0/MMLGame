Read Me:

How to Add a new Category:

Left click -> Add Category button
  
  A dialog box appears asking you to enter a Category name

    Enter a category name
      
      left click -> OK or press enter


How to Upload image:

Left click -> Upload

  A dialog box opens

    Browse to file:

      Left click -> Arrow at top left (this may help you)...
        
        After finding your image to insert:
        
        Left click -> Open at bottom right


How to add a filename and create a caption:
Left click -> Caption
  
  Caption will become highlighted

    Type -> A new caption


Left click -> Save
